# Task

Generate 30 new EPICs in one drilled sector: **Global Trade Finance & Commodity Flows**.

## Objective
Create autonomous, software-only startup ideas that can be built by AI agents in <=3 months, are legal/ethical, and have first-year revenue potential >= $100M.

## Constraints
- No physical product.
- Minimal human interaction.
- Strong ROI framing (time in / cash out).
- Include feasibility score, risk analysis, first-year revenue/users.
- Keep ideas non-trivial, unique, and adoption-friendly.
- Use IDs `EP-0220`..`EP-0249`.

## Deliverables
- `task.md`
- `walkthrough.md`
- `implementation_plan.md`
- 30 EPIC YAML files in `project/epics/`.
