# Task: Autonomous Intellectual Property & Royalty Defense (900-Series)

## Objective
Conceptualize and generate 10 hyper-scale, autonomous business Epics (EP-0900 to EP-0909) targeting the **$100B+ Intellectual Property & Royalty Defense** market. These are "High-Yield Work Horses" designed to find, protect, and monetize "Stolen" or "Missed" IP value via purely agentic software.

## Requirements
- **Goal:** Direct positive cashflow via autonomous discovery, license-matching, and legal refiling.
- **Constraints:** 100% Agency/AI, no human interaction, zero physical products.
- **Scale:** Minimum $100M+ First Year Revenue.
- **Feasibility:** Build-time < 3 months for a solo founder.
- **Killer App Criteria:** Must solve "Hard" IP attribution problems that humans currently ignore or fail to see.

## Success Criteria
- [ ] 10 unique, sophisticated Epics in `project/epics/`.
- [ ] Valid YAML files with comprehensive ROI and Risk data.
