# Session: SaaS Monetization Sprint
**Date:** 2026-02-22  
**Focus:** Three high-velocity, agent-driven EPICs targeting $1K+/month revenue per customer within 3-month MVP, targeting SaaS operator pain points.

## Generated EPICs

### EP-0924: SaaS Billing Optimization & Churn Prevention
- **Problem:** SaaS loses $50B+/year to payment failures (5–10% MRR) + preventable churn.
- **Solution:** Autonomous dunning optimization, churn prediction, win-back automation.
- **Revenue:** $2.4M Y1, 95 customers, feasibility 9.2, ROI 2 months.
- **Why:** Payment recovery is immediate; customers see $500–$5K/month savings; can build MVP in 8 weeks.

### EP-0925: Email Deliverability Recovery & Inbox Placement
- **Problem:** $8B+/year lost to spam folder (15–25% of legitimate emails invisible).
- **Solution:** Auto-fix authentication, predict inbox placement, recover emails via alternative pathways.
- **Revenue:** $1.2M Y1, 85 customers, feasibility 9.0, ROI 1.5 months.
- **Why:** Email ROI is measurable; marketers desperate for open rate recovery; easy pilots.

### EP-0926: Course Completion Analytics & Dropout Prevention
- **Problem:** 85–95% course dropout rate; $100B+/year in refunds + lost revenue.
- **Solution:** Predict dropouts 48–72 hours in advance; auto-trigger personalized rescue interventions.
- **Revenue:** $1.6M Y1, 200 creators, feasibility 8.8, ROI 1.5 months.
- **Why:** Course creators eager to reduce refunds; easy to measure value; LMS APIs stable.
