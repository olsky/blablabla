## Founder Decision Tree

**Decision Point 1: Pick Your Sector**

| Sector | Pros | Cons | Founder Profile |
|--------|------|------|---|
| **SaaS Ops** | Fast GTM, viral potential, horizontal TAM | Competitive, lower ARPU | Tech founder, product obsessed |
| **Enterprise/Logistics** | High ARPU, clear ROI, inbound leads | Long sales, complex integration | Sales/domain hybrid |
| **Healthcare** | Large pain, high margin, regulated moat | Compliance heavy, slower cycles | Healthcare domain expert |
| **IoT/Industrial** | Equipment/infrastructure moat, high ARPU | Capital intensive, technical depth | Manufacturing/EE background |

**Action:** Pick 1 sector where you have unfair advantage (domain knowledge, network, domain belief).

**Decision Point 2: Pick Your Revenue Model**

| Model | GTM Speed | Customer Friction | Revenue Predictability |
|-------|-----------|---|---|
| **Success-Based** (18 EPICs) | FASTEST (days) | LOW (customer wins = founder wins) | VOLATILE (depends on customer success) |

**Action:** For founder stress + team hiring ease, pick **Hybrid** (SaaS + success-based). For fastest revenue, pick **Success-Based**.
