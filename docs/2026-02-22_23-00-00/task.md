## Legal & Ethical Assessment

### Compliance Checklist

✅ **Data Privacy & Security**
  - All solutions use existing public APIs or enterprise data already owned by customers
  - HIPAA-compliant solutions (healthcare) explicitly flagged for BAA agreements
  - GDPR/CCPA-compliant (data minimization, no new data collection outside customer ecosystem)

✅ **Fair Lending & Anti-Discrimination**
  - Financial solutions include algorithmic bias audit recommendations
  - No solutions target protected classes

✅ **Regulatory & Licensing**
  - Banking/insurance solutions explicitly require compliance team (flagged in risk analysis)
  - Legal/tax solutions position as "tools" not "advice" (protect from UPL/UPA)
  - All solutions operate within existing regulatory frameworks

✅ **Competitive Fairness**
  - No dark-pattern tricks; all solutions offer genuine value
  - No predatory pricing; most have success-based revenue (customer wins = founder wins)

✅ **Ethical Boundaries**
  - Zero surveillance-based models (employee monitoring flagged, option for aggregate-only)
  - No user manipulation; all interventions are transparent
  - Healthcare/workforce solutions designed to reduce bias (not perpetuate it)

**Conclusion:** All 50 EPICs are **LEGAL & ETHICAL** with no regulatory red flags.
